Preparing for the 11+

At StemCore, we help to prepare students for the 11+ entrance exams, a crucial step for admission into top grammar and independent schools. Our tailored programs focus on developing key skills in maths, verbal reasoning, non-verbal reasoning, and English.

We have had experience in both taking these exams in the past, and preparing students to take and pass these exams. We have seen positive results through targeted practice, expert guidance, and personalised feedback. We help students build the confidence and competence needed to excel in these challenging exams and secure their place in their desired schools.

How We Teach

At StemCore, our teaching methods are student-centred, personalised, and results-driven. We tailor lessons to meet each student's unique learning style and academic goals, using engaging, interactive techniques to make complex concepts easy to understand. Our approach focuses on:

  • Student-centred learning, with lessons tailored to individual learning styles and academic goals.
  • Engaging, interactive techniques that simplify complex concepts.
  • Building confidence and mastery through regular practice, constructive feedback, and consistent support.
  • Creating a positive learning environment that fosters critical thinking, problem-solving, and academic excellence.

Whether in-person or online, we empower students to reach their full potential and excel in their studies.
